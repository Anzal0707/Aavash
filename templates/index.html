sonogram

Our Sonogram service uses advanced ultrasound technology to provide clear, non-invasive imaging of internal organs and tissues. It’s an essential tool for diagnosing conditions related to the abdomen, heart, and more. With minimal discomfort, we ensure accurate results for timely care.

Performed by skilled technicians, sonograms help detect abnormalities, monitor pregnancy, and evaluate organ health. We prioritize your comfort while delivering precise images to support your medical journey.

  • Safe and painless procedure for detailed results.
  • Clear, high-quality images for precise analysis.
  • Performed by experienced professionals for reliable results.

x-ray

Our X-ray service provides fast, accurate imaging to help diagnose a wide range of conditions. Using minimal radiation, we capture clear images of bones, joints, and organs for effective treatment planning. It's a quick, non-invasive procedure designed to support your healthcare needs.

FAQ

Why Should I choose Medical Health

Choosing Medical Health means prioritizing your well-being with trusted, comprehensive care. We combine advanced medical technology with a team of skilled professionals to deliver exceptional healthcare services. From preventive care to specialized treatments, we are dedicated to meeting your unique needs with compassion and precision. Your health is our top priority, and we strive to provide a safe, supportive environment for every step of your journey toward better health.

What are the Centre’s visiting hours?

Our Centre welcomes visitors during dedicated hours to ensure a comfortable and supportive environment for patients and their loved ones. Visiting hours are thoughtfully designed to balance rest and recovery with the joy of companionship. On Saturdays, many of our doctors are on leave as it is a public holiday, allowing them precious time with their families. However, we are fully active and available on all other days to provide the highest quality care. We kindly encourage you to plan your visits accordingly and appreciate your understanding as we prioritize the well-being of both our patients and healthcare providers

How many visitors are allowed?

To ensure a comfortable and healing environment for all patients, we allow a limited number of visitors at a time. Typically, two visitors per patient are permitted during visiting hours. This helps maintain a peaceful atmosphere while allowing loved ones to offer their support. We kindly ask that you cooperate with our staff to ensure a smooth and respectful visiting experience for everyone.
